Hi! I am a fourth-year B.Tech Computer Science student pursuing an honours in Computer Vision at International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad . I am advised by Dr. Jawahar C. V. and have wonderful friends and collaborators at our lab, CVIT.

At CVIT I am currently working on a research project in lip reading, advised by Dr. Vinay P. Namboodari and Dr. Jawahar C. V. . We have developed personalized models for sentence level lip-reading, with a focus on Deaf speakers, who have difficulty speaking but can still mouth the words, with very unclear / inaudible speech. To test our method and enhance lip-reading accessibility for the Deaf community, we also curated a novel dataset for this Visual Speech Recognition (VSR) task, including out-of-distirbution deaf and accented speakers. (Submitted work is currently under review)

Along with this, I am engaged in an Independent Study in the robotics lab of our college, RRC under Dr. K. Madhava Krishna. Here I am working on developing a system for real-time path planning in autonomous vehicles, prioritizing only essential vehicle interactions, to reduce the computational complexity of the system.

In a seperate research thread under Dr. Charu Sharma and Dr. Avinash Sharma, I am also exploring compressing techniques in 3D gaussian splatting models by exploiting repitions and symmetries in a scene.
